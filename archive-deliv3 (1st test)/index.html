Thanks for agreeing to test our site! Here's what we need.

This site is made for community medical assistants (CMAs) in Yemen. However, we don't know any CMAs. That's where you come in. We want you to do some roleplaying.

Prompt:

You are a medical community assistant in Yemen. A sick patient, potentially with cholera, calls you. Navigate the site and report their symptoms and submit their case to the contact tracing database. The patient information is listed below. Please write the symptoms down somewhere. After you close this popup, you will not be able to see it again.

Your patient is a 35 year old male with symptoms of vomiting and diarrhea.

He is not thirsty and is peeing normally. He lives in Sana'a.

Your patient is a 40 year old female with symptoms of leg cramps, sunken eyes, and dry lips.

She reports feeling restless and being more thirsty than usual. She lives in Abyan.

After you report the cholera case, you'll be asked to fill out a survey asking about your experience. This will help us design the website better. If you are using adblock, please disable it. While we do not host ads, adblock interferes with the technology we are using to research user experience. We will never intentionally collect or publish private info.
